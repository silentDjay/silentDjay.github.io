CAN<i>WALK
Link to Project
My final project for the Front End Engineering course at the Iron Yard. A webapp designed to encourage walking, with its obvious benefits for the walker and her community, by presenting relevant information on weather, physical geography, safety, accessibility, and enjoyability to prospective walkers. Working with one additional front end developer and two Ruby on Rails developers, we built a speedy front end app with AngularJS which heavily used the Google Maps JavaScript API and Places library, and served the back end with a Rails app to store users' information related to walking history and places of interest.
